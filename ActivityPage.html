Activities

Things to Do

There are a large plethora of things to do In heber city, this hidden gem is also a jack of all trades, from waterskiing to snow skiiing, heber has it all.



1. The Train



The train is part of what makes Heber special, you can reserve it for weddings, reserve seats for your family, or if you time it right, go on the polar express. The train runs partially aroun the lake befoer returning back to the original station.



2. Heber Valley Artisan cheese



Heber Valley artisan cheese provides tours on how they make their cheese and their ice cream, both of which taste amazing, it is definitly worth the visit.



3. Bowling



Holiday lanes is a great place to take the family for some bowling. Bowling on a friday can give you the oppertunity for a free game, if the red pin is in front and you get a stike, your game is free, also enjoy their food they make right there in the bowling alley.
